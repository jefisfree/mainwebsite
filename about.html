Jefisfree (Jeff Chiang) is a photographer from Sydney, Australia. He delves in street and urban photography in his spare time. His works convey moody moments that play into a feeling of melancholy. When he has more time, he travels the world aiming to explore every corner of the world on a budget, taking his camera along on the journey, and a never ending appetite for great food. If you have any questions or suggestions, shoot an email to itsjefisfree@gmail.com or find me on my socials.
