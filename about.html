Jefisfree (Jeff Chiang) is from Sydney. He is a photographer who delves in street and urban photography in his spare time. His works convey moody moments that play into a feeling of melancholy. When he has more time, he travels the world aiming to explore every corner of the world. He also eats too much food and always on a food adventure.
